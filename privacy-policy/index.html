Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information RoomReset collects from visitors, how that information is used, and what choices you have as a visitor to this site.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the website located at roomreset.github.io (referred to throughout this document as "RoomReset," "we," "us," or "our"). By visiting and using this website, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use this website.

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to handling any information collected through this site responsibly and transparently. This policy is written in plain language so that you can understand exactly what happens when you visit RoomReset, without needing to parse legal jargon to get to the point.

What Information We Collect

RoomReset does not require visitors to create an account or log in, and we do not actively collect personal information such as your name, email address, or physical address unless you voluntarily provide it by contacting us directly. The information we do collect falls into two categories.

The first category is information collected automatically. When you visit RoomReset, standard web server logs and analytics tools may record information such as your browser type, the device you are using, the pages you visit, how long you spend on each page, the website that referred you to us, and your general geographic location based on your IP address. This information is collected in aggregate and is used solely to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve the content and experience we offer.

The second category is information you provide voluntarily. If you send us an email through the contact link on this site, we receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this only to respond to your enquiry and do not add your address to any mailing list or share it with any third party.

Cookies and Tracking

RoomReset may use cookies, which are small text files stored in your browser, to understand how visitors interact with the site. These are standard analytics cookies and do not store any personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though doing so may affect how some pages on this site display or function.

Third party services that power parts of this site, such as Google Analytics or the Amazon affiliate program, may also set their own cookies when you visit. These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we have no control over. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of Google and Amazon directly if you have concerns about how those services handle your data.

Amazon Associates and Affiliate Links

RoomReset participates in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program that allows us to earn a small commission when visitors click our affiliate links and make a purchase on Amazon. Amazon may use cookies to track purchases made through our links. This tracking is governed by Amazon's own privacy policy, which you can read on the Amazon website. We do not have access to any of your Amazon account information or purchase history.

How We Use Information

Any information collected through this site is used only for the following purposes.

  • To understand which content is most useful to our readers and to improve the quality and relevance of what we publish
  • To respond to direct enquiries sent to us by email
  • To monitor site performance and identify any technical issues that might affect the visitor experience
  • To comply with any legal obligations that may apply to the operation of this website

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes. We do not build user profiles, run advertising campaigns targeting our visitors, or use your data for any purpose beyond those listed above.

Third Party Links

RoomReset contains links to third party websites, primarily Amazon India product pages. Once you leave this site by clicking an external link, this Privacy Policy no longer applies and you are subject to the privacy policy of the website you have visited. We have no control over and accept no responsibility for the content, privacy practices, or security of any third party website.

Children's Privacy

RoomReset is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address below and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in how the site operates or to comply with any new legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we handle information collected through this site.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about how your information is handled on this site, please contact us at cawdimaddi@gmail.com and we will do our best to respond promptly.
