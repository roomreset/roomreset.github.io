Affiliate Disclosure

We believe in being fully transparent about how RoomReset earns money and how that does and does not influence the recommendations you read on this site.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

RoomReset is a participant in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to allow websites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon.in. When you click an affiliate link on this site and make a qualifying purchase on Amazon, we earn a small commission at no additional cost to you whatsoever.

This disclosure page exists because we believe you deserve to know exactly how this site is funded and how that funding does and does not affect what we write. We take our editorial independence seriously, and we want to explain in plain terms what that means in practice for you as a reader.

What an Affiliate Link Is

An affiliate link is a standard product link that contains a small tracking code identifying RoomReset as the source of the referral. When you click one of these links and visit Amazon, a cookie is stored in your browser for a period of time. If you make a purchase during that window, Amazon attributes the sale to RoomReset and pays us a percentage of the sale value as a commission. The price you pay for the product is exactly the same whether you use an affiliate link or search for the product directly on Amazon yourself. The commission comes from Amazon's own margin, not from any additional charge to you.

Our Amazon Associates tracking tag is ecoessentia0b-21. You may notice this tag appearing in the URL of links on this site. This is normal and simply identifies us as the referring publisher for the purposes of commission tracking.

How This Affects Our Recommendations

The short answer is that our affiliate relationships do not change which products we recommend. We have a straightforward editorial policy: a product gets recommended on RoomReset because it genuinely suits the purpose described in the article, has a price that represents good value for what you are getting, and has a track record of positive reviews from real buyers. A product that fails any one of those tests does not appear in our content, regardless of the commission rate it carries.

We also want to be direct about something that is often glossed over on other sites. Some affiliate programs pay significantly higher commissions than others. We do not let this influence which products we place more prominently in our articles. A product that earns us a lower commission but is genuinely the best option for our readers will always be recommended over a higher commission product that is a worse fit. Our long term credibility depends on readers being able to trust our recommendations, and that trust is worth far more to us than any incremental difference in commission rates.

Which Links Are Affiliate Links

On RoomReset, most product links that point to Amazon are affiliate links. Rather than labeling every individual link throughout every article, we disclose this practice at the site level through this page and with a brief note within each article that contains product recommendations. This is standard industry practice and is in line with the disclosure requirements set by the Amazon Associates Program and applicable advertising guidelines.

Not every link on this site is an affiliate link. Links to other websites, reference sources, or non-Amazon destinations are standard links with no tracking component.

Our Commitment to You

We want RoomReset to be a site you can return to when you need honest, practical home decor advice, with the confidence that what you read is driven by genuine research rather than by which products pay us the most. The following commitments guide every piece of content we publish.

  • We only recommend products we believe genuinely serve the purpose described in the article
  • We never accept payment from brands or manufacturers in exchange for positive coverage
  • We never let commission rates determine which products appear in our top recommendations
  • We update our recommendations when better products become available or when previously recommended products receive consistently negative reviews
  • We are transparent about the fact that this site earns income through affiliate commissions and do not attempt to obscure or minimize that fact
