Who we are

The story behind
RoomReset

A home decor guide built for people who want their space to feel calm, beautiful, and completely their own, without needing a large budget or a designer to get there.

RoomReset was created out of a simple frustration: most home decor content online is either aimed at people with unlimited budgets, or it is so generic it could apply to any room in any country and ends up being useful to nobody in particular.

We wanted something more honest than that. A guide that starts from the reality most people are actually living in, a modest amount of money, a small or imperfect space, and a genuine desire to make it feel better without turning it into a renovation project.

Every article on this site is written with one question in mind: what is the single most effective thing someone can do to improve this room today, with what they can reasonably afford? That question shapes everything from the products we recommend to the way we explain each idea. We never recommend something because it looks good in a photoshoot. We recommend it because it genuinely earns its place in a real room.

Our approach

Every recommendation earns its place

Every product recommendation on RoomReset goes through the same basic test before it makes it onto the site. Does it do what it claims to do? Does it look good in a real home setting rather than just a staged photo? Is the price genuinely reasonable for what you are getting? Does it have enough reviews from real buyers to give us confidence it holds up over time?

Products that pass all four questions make the cut. Products that fail any one of them do not appear here, regardless of commission rates or brand partnerships.

Our process

How every recommendation is made

01

Research the space

Every article begins with a clear understanding of the specific room, its typical constraints, and what most people actually struggle with in that space.

02

Test the products

We look for products with genuine reviews, real use photos, and a track record of holding up over time before any recommendation is made.

03

Check the price

Every product is assessed for value at its actual price point. We never recommend something just because it looks good in a photoshoot.

04

Update regularly

Products change, prices shift, and better options appear. We treat every article as a living document and revisit recommendations regularly.
